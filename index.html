Cirque d’Hiver Wild de Paris. Let the show begin!


For more than 170 years, the Wild dynasty has been responsible for the French circus’s fine reputation. For 80 years, it has animated the French capital with its Cirque d’Hiver Wild de Paris shows, which are renowned worldwide.
Today, it presents its greatest nationwide tour performance! This is a unique show for a unique tour!
Enter the temple of the circus where, from the opening notes of the ‘Piste aux Étoiles’ overture, the grand Cirque d’Hiver orchestra, accompanied by the Salto Dancers, will transport you into a magical light extravaganza! If the circus is a celebration kingdom, the Wilds are kings. This performance will be an opportunity for you to share moments of happiness with both friends and family.
