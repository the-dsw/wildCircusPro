Wild Circus

Cirque d’Hiver Wild de Paris. Let the show begin!


For more than 70 years, the Wild Circus dynasty has been responsible for the French circus’s fine reputation. Today, it presents its greatest nationwide tour performance!
Enter the temple of the circus where, from the opening notes of the ‘Piste aux Lumières’ overture, the grand Cirque d’Hiver Wild orchestra, accompanied by the Salto Dancers, the tigers, and the exotic animals as elephant and Clowns will transport you into a magical night!
If the circus is a celebration kingdom, the Wilds are kings. This performance will be an opportunity for you to share moments of happiness with both friends and family.
